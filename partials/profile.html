Design Philosophy

I stick with very foundation in doing the illustrations. Colors and composition. I try to find best way to appeal the audience with variety in colors yet, give them plenty of comfort in eyes that never bore them.

Inspirations

I adore traditional illustrator. In colors, I admire Norman Rockwell. In drawings, I love Jean-Jacques Sempe. (Look it up Nicholas series) I am also deeply influenced by one of teachers of mine Natalie Nascencio.

Materials

For colors, I like to use oils, gouahce, watercolors. For drawings, I use ink, pen, and pencil.
